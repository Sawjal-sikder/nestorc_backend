PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated November 17, 2025

This Privacy Notice for Bolt Scooters LLC ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Download and use our mobile application (Bolt Scooter), or any other application of ours that links to this Privacy Notice

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register, request information, participate in activities, or contact us.

Personal Information Provided by You:

  • Names
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Authentication data

Sensitive Information: We do not process sensitive information.

Social Media Login Data: If you register using Facebook, X, etc., we collect certain profile information. See “How do we handle your social logins?”

Application Data (Optional):

  • Geolocation Data — If you grant permission, we may track location for app functionality.

All personal information must be true and accurate, and you must notify us of any changes.
